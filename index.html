Deployment programs

Three programs - for every stage of company growth

Each program is a specific scope of work and a package of technologies matched to the company's size and risk. No vague talk - you know what you get and who it is for.

Getting started · 1-10 employees

M365 Admin

A simple program to deploy a secure work environment. Every month you get new security settings - your company grows in protection step by step.

Who it is for Micro companies and starting teams of 1-10 people. You have no IT, you do not want "big theory" - you want things to be secure and simply work.
  • Email security - anti-phishing, anti-spoof, DKIM/DMARC
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts
  • Advanced antivirus for computers - Defender for Business
  • Monthly package of security improvements
Ask about M365 Admin
Growing · 10-50 employees

M365 Cyber

A deployment plan for elevated security for growing companies. Full control of access, devices and communication - with no gaps in your security policies.

Who it is for Small and medium companies of 10-50 people. They run on Microsoft 365 but know that without policies and device management things get risky.
  • Conditional Access to Microsoft 365
  • User protection - Identity Protection, anti-phishing
  • Managed computers (Intune) - policies, encryption, BYOD
  • Phone security - MAM/MDM, separation of company data
Ask about M365 Cyber
Scaling · 50-300 employees

M365 Expert

Top-class security for Microsoft 365 together with security monitoring. Full observability of your environment - you know what is happening before something goes wrong.

Who it is for Medium companies of 50-300 people. Regulatory requirements (GDPR, NIS2, industry-specific), many mobile workers, sensitive data - control must be continuous.
  • Advanced data protection - Purview, DLP, classification labels
  • Security monitoring - Defender XDR, alerts, reports
  • Full cloud administration - identities, devices, apps
  • Managed changes and security reviews
